Moro Rock - Kings Canyon
  • Back to Hiking Summary

September 14, 2023

We completed this hike in September while visiting both Sequoia and Kings Canyon National Parks. At only .5 miles out and back, but encompassing almost 400 steps of climbing, this short hike is nevertheless a fun one.

The stairs wrap alongside the granite dome to the very top. Here, the vantage point of Sequoia and the Sierra Nevada was spectacular, although fairly hazy on that day due to smoke from wildfires burning in the area beyond.

Scenes from the hike: Pictures showing the granite dome, handrails along the trail, and views to the mountains beyond.
