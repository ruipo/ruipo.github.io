Ocean Cay - The Bahamas
  • Back to Hiking Summary

May 10, 2023

Perhaps the most unique “hike” we completed this past year was at Ocean Cay, a marine reserve and island in the Bahamas owned by MSC Cruises (we took our first cruise ever in May!). We had most of the day just to explore the island, including its beaches, lighthouses, and sandy trails. The ocean was such a stunning blue color, and we enjoyed laying on the beaches to watch the waves come in.

That evening, we were treated to a beautiful sunset and lighthouse lights show.

Scenes from the hike: initial docking at the island; a view of our cruise ship from the island; view overlooking the island; views of the cruise ship; the main lighthouse; Christine admiring the view from the ship balcony; a Caribbean sunset!
