General Grant - Sequoia
  • Back to Hiking Summary

September 14, 2023

This was a great short walk to the General Grant sequoia tree in Sequoia National Park. By total volume of wood, this massive tree is supposedly the second-largest tree. It is 268 feet tall with a diameter of 33 feet, and is an impressive 1,700 years old.

An easy .5 mile walk leads to the tree, with plenty of other sequoia trees surrounding it to gaze up at. It was hard to get a sense of scale size until we were standing close enough to the giants!
