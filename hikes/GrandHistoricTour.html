Grand Historic Tour - Mammoth Cave
  • Back to Hiking Summary

August 26, 2023

Although different from our traditional hikes, we wanted to include our 4-mile trek through just a small portion of the much larger network of caves within Mammoth Cave National Park. Along for the adventure were our good friends Paige and Sarah.

Upon entering the caves, we were struck by how chilly the air felt inside, considering it was late August in Kentucky! We had an awesome and knowledgeable tour guide leading our group and learned about much of the geological history of the caves - a bonus to the hike itself. Lights lit up much of the cave paths and we were also surprised by the sheer size of the rooms and caverns within. We admired the underground stalagmites and stalactites, huge boulders, old engravings in the rock, pools of water, and even enjoyed the tight twists and turns through close quarters of rock.

Mammoth Cave was unlike any place we’ve visited before and did not disappoint!
