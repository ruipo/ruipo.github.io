Half Dome - Yosemite
  • Back to Hiking Summary

September 12, 2023

With nearly 5,000 feet of total elevation gain and 20 miles round-trip in one day, this was no doubt the hardest hike we’ve completed to date (map and elevation plot show ascent only). In order to accomplish this, we needed to start our day hiking at 3:30 am in the dark from Curry Village. Several rest breaks and refueling breaks were needed along the way, and thankfully, the weather was cooperative that day.

We also had our good friend David (and his plethora of snacks!), join us for this hike. When we finally reached the summit of Half Dome around 10:00am, we were pretty exhausted already and our legs felt like jello. The views of the landscape around us however made it all worth it!

A detour during the descent extended our hike by a couple of miles and our legs were incredibly achy by the time we reached our camp again. Needless to say, we slept well that night!
