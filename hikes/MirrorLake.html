Mirror Lake - Yosemite
  • Back to Hiking Summary

September 11, 2023

We completed this hike the day before Half Dome, consisting of a leisurely 5-miles total, out and back trail to the namesake Mirror Lake. The trail begins from Yosemite Village and follows a lovely walking/biking trail all the way to the lake. We were treated to stunning views of Tenaya Canyon and were able to gaze up from the base of Half Dome in the reflection of the lake. The massive granite walls around us were otherworldly!
